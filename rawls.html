The Difference Principle

The difference principle is the justification for inequality and the motivation behind the redistributive policies of Rawls' political conception of justice as fairness.


Ode to a Soft Tree: A Poem dedicated to that late John Rawls




Rawls & Impiety

The great John Rawls after his
Restatement of the theory of distributive justice
As fairness “passed away” (reincarnated) into a slow
Growing Japanese Grey Elm
Visiting me in a dream last night where
He was inadvertently (and irreparably) compressed by
An unwitting and large-bottomed so-and-so
Trying to sit down.

Maximin

The maximin principle is not unique to Rawls, but it does play an important role in characterizing the goal of redistributive policies in justice as fairness.
